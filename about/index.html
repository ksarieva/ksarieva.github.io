About me

I'm a PhD student in Hertie Institute for Clinical Brain Research, Tübingen, Germany supervised by Dr. Simone Mayer.
In my research, I use brain region-specific organoids and single-cell transcriptomics to understand molecular mechanisms underlying environmental and genetic perturbations in human brain development.

During my Master's studies, I worked in Euler lab in the Center for Integrative Neuroscience (CIN), Tübingen, Germany supervised by Dr. Timm Schubert. There, I applied STED super-resolution microscopy to image inner retina in whole-mount preparations.
